## Objective
Investigate unauthorized or unusual administrator actions.

---

## Detection Signals
- Admin login outside normal hours
- Privilege escalation alerts
- Account changes

---

## Initial Triage Steps
1. Confirm admin identity
2. Verify business justification
3. Check related activity

---

## Investigation Workflow
- Review Windows security logs
- Analyze privilege changes
- Correlate timeline

---

## Severity Assessment
High risk due to elevated privileges

---

## Containment Actions
- Temporarily suspend admin account
- Escalate to security leadership

---

## Recovery Actions
- Audit system changes
- Restore permissions

---

## Documentation Requirements
Detailed admin timeline required.

---

## Lessons Learned
Improve privilege monitoring.
